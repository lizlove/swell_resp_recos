Takeaways

Our advice on what to spend

Although we understand how essential having a diaper bag is, it doesn’t need to be a large purchase. Most diaper bags will have similar functionality, so it’s really up to you whether you’re looking for one that’s more brand-specific or more comfortable and casual.

What to consider

  • Chances are you’ll be carrying this around everywhere you go, so you want to look for a bag that’s made of easy-to-clean material, like nylon or cotton.
  • Many parents tend to say that the comfort level of the straps is really important.
  • Storage space is great, but what is even more helpful is a bag that helps you organize your stuff.
  • You should be able to easily access your bag with one hand, this will come in handy when you're changing your baby and only have one free hand.
  • Velcro closures were one of the biggest pet peeves of parents who tested different bags.
